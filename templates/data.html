Looking at the data!

The distribution of points given by expert wine reviewers by prices (USD) for all 130k bottles. We can already see a couple of wines standing out as being very well reviewed and priced much higher than other bottles.

If we take a closer look at the data, zooming in on specific regions of France for example, we see that bordeaux dominates the region of its namesake.

Surprisingly Chardonnay is in fact the most expensive and highest rated wine despite this being the region of Champagne!
